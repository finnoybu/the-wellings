After the completion of the Wellings franchise, we should consider growing into a larger franchise of inclusive families with inclusive images.

Selecting names and images of a diverse background will help broaden the scope and provide a better representation of people

Inclusive images should feature a diverse mix of backgrounds—Black/African American, Hispanic/Latinx, Asian, Middle Eastern, Indigenous/Native American, and Caucasian/White—while reflecting varied ages, genders, sexual orientations, body types, and abilities. Authenticity is key, avoiding tokenism by depicting realistic, non-stereotyped, and intersectional representations of people.
