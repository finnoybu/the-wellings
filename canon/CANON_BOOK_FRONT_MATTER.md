# Canon Book Front Matter

This document defines the canonical front matter architecture for all books
in *The Wellings* franchise.

This architecture applies to all sets, all books, and all editions.

Front matter is standardized and is not authored per book except where
explicitly noted.

---

## Scope

- Applies to print and digital editions
- Governs physical page order prior to story content
- Distinguishes exterior cover from interior pagination
- Defines recto/verso and blank-page rules

---

## Front Matter Sequence

### 1. Front Cover (Exterior)

- Franchise Title: *The Wellings*
- Book Title
- Full illustration
- Author name
- Exterior only
- **Not counted as an interior page**

---

### 2. Dedication Poem Page (Recto)

- Illustration: one character chasing a butterfly
- Short poem
  - Must be public-domain, licensed, or original
- No story text

---

### 3. Blank Page (Verso)

- Back of dedication poem page

---

### 4. Interior Title Page (Recto)

Interior title page may reuse cover composition in simplified form.

Canonical contents:

- Franchise Title
- Book Title
- Small version of the cover illustration
- Author name
- Edition (e.g., FIRST EDITION)
- Publisher logo (Rogaland COA cross)
- Publisher name (Rogaland House)

---

### 5. Copyright Page (Verso)

- Standard copyright and publication information
- No illustration

---

### 6. Set / Series Page (Recto)

Displays the relationship between:

- *The Wellings* (franchise)
- Current set
- Current book

No story content appears on this page.

---

### 7. Blank Page (Verso)

- Back of set / series page

---

## Story Start Rule

- Story content begins only after completion of front matter
- Story Page 1 must begin on a recto page
- Manuscripts represent story pages only
